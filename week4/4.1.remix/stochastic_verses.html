This poem is Anna Hollrah's adaptation of Stochastic Texts by Nick Monfort.

I used wording related to planting, cultivating, and growing things on a farm or in a garden. Using the randomness of the Stochastic Pattern helped to create some very unique sentances about gardening that are inspiring and motivational to plant and grow our own food. There are some of the sentances that do make you thinking is that true - can you find them?
